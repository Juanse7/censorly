Why Censorly

Redaction that actually means it.

Six things Censorly does differently from a black box drawn in your Photos app.

01 / PIXELS

Pixels permanently destroyed

Redactions are irreversible. The area is overwritten at the pixel level, not covered with a box that can be removed later.

02 / OFFLINE

100% offline

Everything happens on your device. Censorly never needs an internet connection — your documents never leave your hands.

03 / FACES

Facial recognition

Censorly finds and permanently removes faces from your documents automatically, so you don't have to hunt for every one by hand.

04 / RELIABILITY

Human error free

Popular redaction methods fail silently — boxes that can be dragged away, text that was never truly erased. Censorly removes that risk.

05 / METADATA

Metadata removal

Hidden metadata in images and PDFs can expose location, device, and timestamps. Censorly strips it before you save the file.

06 / PRECISION

Zoom and scroll

Zoom into dense documents and scroll freely, so small print and tucked-away details don't slip past your redactions.
